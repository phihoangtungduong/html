NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

Kinds of noun

Nouns are classified as follows:

  • Phoper:Is is the name of.....
  • Common:Is is the name possesesed..
  • Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

Kinds of pronouns

Pronouns are classified as follows::

  • Personal pronouns: It refers toa specific noun and used as a subsitute of the noun
  • Interrogative pronouns:It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing
  • Indefinite pronouns: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown
  • Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
  • Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it
  • Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
